Discography

Thank Me Later (2010)

"Thank Me Later" is Drake's debut studio album, released on June 15, 2010. It debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 and was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). The album features collaborations with artists like Jay-Z, Kanye West, and Lil Wayne.

Take Care (2011)

"Take Care" is Drake's second studio album, released on November 15, 2011. The album won the Grammy Award for Best Rap Album at the 55th Annual Grammy Awards and features collaborations with Rihanna, Nicki Minaj, and Kendrick Lamar, among others.

Nothing Was the Same (2013)

Released on September 24, 2013, "Nothing Was the Same" is Drake's third studio album. It spawned the hit singles "Started from the Bottom" and "Hold On, We're Going Home". The album was praised for its intricate lyricism and emotional depth.

Views (2016)

"Views" is Drake's fourth studio album, released on April 29, 2016. It sat atop the Billboard 200 chart for 13 nonconsecutive weeks and features hit singles like "Hotline Bling", "One Dance", and "Too Good".

Scorpion (2018)

Released on June 29, 2018, "Scorpion" is a double album consisting of 25 tracks. It includes hits like "God's Plan", "In My Feelings", and "Nice for What". The album broke several streaming records.

Certified Lover Boy (2021)

Drake's sixth studio album, "Certified Lover Boy", was released on September 3, 2021. The album features collaborations with artists like Jay-Z, Travis Scott, and Kid Cudi, and includes tracks like "Way 2 Sexy" and "Girls Want Girls".

Awards

Drake, a highly acclaimed artist, has received numerous awards throughout his prolific career. Some of the notable accolades include:

  • Grammy Awards: Drake has won several Grammy Awards, including Best Rap Album for "Take Care" and Best Rap Song for "God's Plan".
  • Billboard Music Awards: Drake holds the record for the most Billboard Music Awards won by an artist, with a total of 27 awards as of 2021.
  • Junos Awards: As a Canadian artist, Drake has received several Juno Awards, recognizing his significant contribution to the Canadian music industry.
  • MTV Video Music Awards: Drake has also won several MTV Video Music Awards, including Best Hip-Hop Video for "HYFR" and Best Male Video for "Hold On, We're Going Home".
  • BRIT Awards: Drake has received the BRIT Award for International Male Solo Artist multiple times, recognizing his influence on the global music scene.

Besides these, Drake has also received honors from the American Music Awards, BET Awards, and many other prestigious platforms, establishing him as one of the most awarded artists in the music industry.
